Services

Pickups sized to your load

Whether it's a few tires from the garage or a monthly haul from a service bay, we scope the pickup to what you actually have — you tell us the count when you book.

One-time

Single pickup

For a one-off batch — a garage clear-out, a few tires left after a swap, or a small lot cleanup. Tell us the count and address and we'll schedule a visit.

Recurring

Monthly service

For shops and properties that generate tires steadily. Set your estimated monthly volume when you book and we'll build a standing pickup schedule around it.

Bulk

Large-volume hauls

Bigger loads — commercial, agricultural, or heavy truck tires — get routed to the right equipment and disposal path. Flag the volume in your request.

What we take

Most tire types, most volumes

  • Passenger and light-truck tires, with or without rims
  • Commercial and heavy-truck tires
  • Motorcycle and small equipment tires
  • Mixed loads — you don't need to sort by type before we arrive
How pricing works

Based on your count, not a flat guess

Because loads vary a lot — a few tires from a household versus a shop's monthly volume — we quote after you submit your count and address, not before. That's the main reason the booking form asks for both.
