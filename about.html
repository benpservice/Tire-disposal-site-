About

Tires shouldn't be your problem to store

Tire Disposal & Distribution picks up used tires and routes them into proper recycling instead of a landfill or a back lot — so shops, properties, and households don't have to sit on a growing pile.

Why it matters

Old tires don't break down on their own

Left alone, tires take up space, collect water, and become a hazard rather than just an eyesore. Getting them into a proper recycling stream — instead of a landfill or an illegal dump — is the responsible way to close out a tire's life.

How we operate
  • Licensed and insured pickup and hauling
  • Tires routed to recycling, not landfill
  • Pickups scoped to your actual count — no minimums that don't fit you
  • Recurring service available for shops and properties with steady volume
